Finally, a simple way to sync your WordPress users to your Minecraft server. The main purpose of this plugin is to force a user to register on your WordPress (and soon BuddyPress) website before they can play on your server. Unlike other plugins that are developed for Forums and have lots and lots of complicated settings, this plugin will be targeted at Word/Buddy press ONLY.

Possible Uses:

Force Members to register for your site before they can play Make users read the rules Unlock permissions Configurable settings:

Database Host, Port, Username, and Password WordPress Database name User Table What to do with un-registered players (kick, freeze, restrict, etc...)
